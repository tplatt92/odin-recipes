Pizza Recipe

Description

Trust me, once you see how simple and tasty this pizza dough recipe is you won't want takeaways

Ingredients

Dough

  • 800g strong white bread flour
  • 200g fine semolina flour
  • 1tsp fine seat salt
  • 2 satchets dried yeast
  • 1 tsp golden caster sugar

Tomato Sauce

  • 1 bunch fresh basil
  • 400g plum tomatoes
  • olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic

Toppings

  • 85g mazzarella cheese
